Overview

Site Name: Benncima Chamber of Commerce

I chose this website because it represents the local chamber of commerce in my region. Upon examining the site, I noticed its simplicity and the potential for a more modern and appealing design. Additionally, I observed a lack of substantial content on the site.

Site Purpose

The Purpose of Benin City Chamber of Commerce is to promote and protect the interests of its members and Edo State business community at large through public policy advocacy, creation and facilitation of trade, investment and general business opportunities, provision of business development services and observance of highest standard of business ethics among its members.
Benin Chamber of Commerce through its regular advocacy programme sensitizes the State Government towards offering special incentives to investors who show genuine interest in the agro and agro-allied subsector especially processing, preservation, and export of local agricultural produce. In a bid towards making Edo State a hub of economic activities in Nigeria especially because of its centrality and comparative advantage in location, the Chamber sensitizes members and the business community on local content development. With a little spur from investors, the farmers will be able to expand their output and improve on the standard of raw materials available for agro-industry.

Scenarios

Below are some scenarios that provide questions to answers.

  1. I'm Interested in agro-allied investment in Edo State. What incentives does the Chamber offer? Where can I find details on opportunities?
  2. I'm a local farmer looking to expand in Edo State. How does the Chamber collaborate with farmers? Any ongoing programs for yield improvement and better prices?
  3. I'm considering mining investment in Edo. Can the Chamber provide info on the industry's state, potential investment areas, and available support or partnerships?
